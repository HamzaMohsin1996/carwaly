Tom Clark

Scenario: Tom Clark is an adventurous cyclist from Garmisch who uses the Cyclosphere app and AR glasses for a seamless, safe, and enhanced riding experience.

Expectations:

Discover new trails, improve performance, stay safe with real-time hazard notifications, and enjoy a smooth integration with AR technology.

01 Starting the Day

Action: Wake up and plan the ride

Touch Points: Opens the Cyclosphere app on his phone

02 Using the App to Find a Route

Action: Search for new trails using the Route Finder, sync selected trail to AR glasses

Touch Points: Browses through trails, reads user reviews, checks difficulty levels, confirms route, synchronizes it to glasses

03 Preparing for the Ride

Action: Customize safety settings

Touch Points: Sets sound alerts, haptic feedback, updates emergency contact info

04 During the Ride

Action: Ride with real-time navigation and hazard alerts, monitor performance metrics

Touch Points: Follows real-time navigation cues, receives hazard notifications, checks speed, distance, elevation on AR display

05 Post-Ride Analysis

Action: Review performance metrics

Touch Points: Opens post-ride summary with charts and graphs

06 Reflecting and Sharing

Action: Reflect on achievements and plan the next ride

Touch Points: Reviews performance trends, sets new goals, plans the next adventure
